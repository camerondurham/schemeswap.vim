INTRODUCTION                                    *schemeswap*

Automatically change your Vim colorscheme (and other stuff) at a set time.  This
doc is a work in progress...

                                                *schemeswap-:BG*
:BG                     Toggle colorscheme settings from light to dark.

ABOUT                                           *schemeswap-about*
